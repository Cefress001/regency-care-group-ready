The Benefits of Partnering With Us

Access to Premium Clients

We work with families who understand and respect the value of high-quality care. Let us connect you with rewarding, long-term positions where your skills are truly appreciated.

Competitive, Direct-Hire Wages

Our model facilitates a direct-hire relationship between you and the family. This means no agency markups, allowing families to offer you a more competitive and sustainable living wage.

Focus on What You Do Best: Care

Spend your time providing excellent care, not searching for clients. We handle the marketing, initial screening, and introductions, so you can focus on your passion.

Our Standards of Excellence

We are seeking caregivers who are committed to the highest standards of professionalism and compassion. Our minimum requirements include:

  • A minimum of 5 years of professional caregiving experience
  • Verifiable, glowing references from past clients/employers
  • Ability to pass a comprehensive national background check
  • Relevant certifications (e.g., CNA, HHA) are highly preferred
  • Excellent communication skills and a professional demeanor
  • A genuine passion for providing empathetic and dignified care
