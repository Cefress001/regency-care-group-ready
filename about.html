About Regency Care Group

Care that feels less like a service and more like family.

Our Philosophy: A Commitment to Human Connection

Regency Care Group was born from a simple yet profound belief: finding in-home care should be a human-centered experience, not a transactional one. In a world of large, impersonal agencies and the daunting risks of unvetted private hiring, we saw a critical need for a more thoughtful, personalized approach. We are dedicated to bridging that gap, offering a concierge service that prioritizes safety, compatibility, and genuine human connection above all else.

Our philosophy is rooted in the understanding that a caregiver is more than just a helper; they become an integral part of a family's life. That's why we invest so much time in getting to know both our clients and our caregivers on a deeper level. We believe that the best care relationships are built on a foundation of trust, respect, and shared values.

A Message from Our Founder

"Having navigated the complexities of finding care for my own family, I knew there had to be a better way. A way that honored the dignity of our loved ones and gave families true peace of mind. That is the promise behind Regency Care Group."

With a deep, personal commitment to compassionate care, our founder established Regency Care Group to address the shortcomings of the traditional care industry. Witnessing firsthand the challenges families face—from the sterile, one-size-fits-all approach of large agencies to the anxieties of hiring a stranger online—the mission became clear: to create a service that was both rigorously safe and deeply personal.

Every aspect of our process, from our multi-layered vetting system to our intuitive matching protocol, is infused with the dedication to get it right. We don't just fill a position; we build relationships. Every match we make is a reflection of our unwavering commitment to enhancing the quality of life for our clients and their families, ensuring safety, promoting independence, and fostering genuine companionship.

Our Core Values

Dignity & Respect

We believe that every individual deserves to be treated with the utmost respect. Our approach to care is centered on preserving the dignity and independence of every client we serve.

Trust & Transparency

We operate with complete transparency. From our vetting process to our fee structure, we provide clear, honest communication to build a foundation of trust with every family.

Compassion & Empathy

Our work is driven by a genuine sense of compassion. We lead with heart, seeking to understand the emotional needs of our clients and their families to provide truly empathetic support.
