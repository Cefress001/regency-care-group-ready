Frequently Asked Questions

A caregiver referral service, or registry, acts as a matchmaker connecting families directly with professional, independent caregivers. Unlike a traditional home care agency where caregivers are employees of the company, our service empowers you to become the employer. We handle the intensive work of sourcing, screening, and vetting qualified candidates, presenting you with a selection of caregivers who match your specific needs and preferences. This model provides you with greater control, consistency, and a direct relationship with your chosen caregiver.

The key differences lie in control, cost, and consistency. With a referral service, you hire directly, giving you full control over care schedules, daily tasks, and compensation. This direct-employer model eliminates agency overhead, often resulting in 30-40% cost savings for families. Furthermore, it fosters a more stable and personal relationship with your caregiver, reducing the turnover that is common with agency-assigned staff. While online marketplaces leave you to vet candidates on your own, we provide the professional screening and ongoing support to ensure a safe and successful match.

Our proprietary vetting process is designed to ensure the highest standards of safety, skill, and professionalism. Every candidate undergoes a multi-step evaluation:

  • Experience Verification: A thorough review of their professional history and qualifications.
  • In-Depth Interviews: A behavioral interview with our team to assess their expertise, communication skills, and compassionate approach.
  • Reference Checks: We speak directly with previous employers to confirm reliability, integrity, and performance.
  • Background Coordination: Before a final offer is made, we coordinate a comprehensive third-party background check, including criminal records and driving history.

As a household employer, you are responsible for managing payroll, withholding taxes (Social Security, Medicare), and securing a workers' compensation insurance policy. While this may sound complex, we simplify it completely. We partner with industry-leading payroll services like HomePay by Care.com, which automate all tax filings, payroll processing, and compliance. They make managing the financial side of employment effortless, allowing you to focus on the care itself.

Our pricing structure is transparent and designed for value. You pay a one-time referral fee for our comprehensive search and vetting service. This fee covers the entire process of finding and matching you with a qualified caregiver. The caregiver's hourly rate is then paid directly to them, without any ongoing agency markups. This direct-pay model is the primary driver of the significant cost savings our clients experience compared to traditional agencies.

Your long-term satisfaction is our highest priority. We pride ourselves on making exceptional matches, but we understand that the ideal fit is crucial. That's why we offer a 90-day replacement guarantee. If, for any reason, the relationship with your caregiver does not meet your expectations within the first 90 days, we will conduct a new, comprehensive search to find a better match at no additional referral cost to you.
