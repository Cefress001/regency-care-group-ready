Frequently Asked Questions

A traditional home care agency employs caregivers directly, managing their payroll, scheduling, and supervision. As a referral agency, we specialize in sourcing, vetting, and matching you with independent caregivers whom you then hire directly. This model offers greater control and flexibility for your family and typically results in significant cost savings (30-40%) by eliminating agency overhead.

While every search is unique, our process is designed for efficiency without compromising on quality. After the initial discovery call, we can typically present you with 2-3 highly qualified candidates for interviews within 7-10 days.

We charge a one-time referral fee for our comprehensive sourcing, vetting, and matching service. This fee is a fraction of the long-term savings you'll achieve compared to a traditional agency. The caregiver's hourly rate is paid directly by you to the caregiver, and we provide guidance on competitive rates in our area.

We pride ourselves on our 98% successful match rate. However, we understand that circumstances can change. We offer a 90-day guarantee, during which we will conduct a new search at no additional cost to ensure we find the perfect fit for your family.
